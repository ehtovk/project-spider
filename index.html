History

Spider-Man is a superhero created by the writer and editor Stan Lee. In Spider-Man's first story, an american teenager called Peter Parker went to a public demonstration of how to handle a nuclear waste. During the demonstration, a spider was infected by the nuclear waste and fell his hand. Then, the spider bites Peter and die in the next minute. As a result of the bite, he gains speed, superhuman strength, hability to climb walls and shoot web like a spider.
